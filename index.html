Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

Hans Studio builds mobile applications designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy applies to all our apps including Dose, Flux, Bloom, Drift, and Ember.

Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • All data stays on your device

Data Storage

Any data you create is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children.
